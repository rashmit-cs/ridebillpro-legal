Features

Everything a taxi operator actually needs

Designed around real Indian cab billing — not a generic invoice app with taxi labels slapped on.

📄
GST-Compliant Invoices
Optional GST (5%) on corporate bills. Includes GSTIN, SAC code, and proper tax breakup. Personal trips stay GST-free.
📦
Package Rate Billing
Fixed package (km + hr bundle), extra km rate, extra hour rate — exactly how physical bill books work in India.
➕
All Extra Charges
Toll, parking, waiting, night charge, airport fee, ferry, hill surcharge, driver bata, state tax, luggage, and more.
📋
Trip Log & CSV Export
Mirrors your Excel logbook — Date, Customer, Drop Point, Time, Vehicle No., Amount. Export CSV anytime.
🔒
Offline & Private
No cloud, no login, no Firebase. All data stays on your device. Works without internet — even in remote hills.
🖨️
PDF & Print Ready
Share professional PDF invoices via WhatsApp or print directly. Clean, company-branded layout.
