Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Simple, powerful tools designed for daily transport operations in India.

🧾

GST-Ready Invoices

Generate professional invoices with CGST, SGST, IGST breakdown. Auto-calculated totals. Print or share as PDF.

📄

PDF Export

Export any bill as a clean, formatted PDF and share via WhatsApp, Email, or save to your device. Available on Pro plans.

📋

Trip Log Management

Keep a quick daily logbook of every trip — passenger name, pickup, drop, vehicle, and fare.

🚗

Fleet Management

Add and manage all your vehicles in one place. Track trip history per vehicle with ease.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

See total revenue, monthly trends, average bill value, top vehicles, and GST summaries at a glance.

🏢

Company Profile

Store your company name, GSTIN, logo, UPI ID, and bank details. Auto-filled on every invoice.

📱

100% Offline

All data lives on your phone using Room Database. No internet, no cloud, no subscription lock-in for your data.

Delete All Data

Want a fresh start? Delete all bills, trip logs, and settings in one tap. You own your data completely.
