About RideBill Pro

Built for transport professionals who need simple, reliable billing on the go

Our Story

Why We Built This

Transport operators across India — taxi drivers, cab owners, bus operators, and fleet managers — often track their billing in physical notebooks or messy spreadsheets. Generating a GST-compliant invoice meant opening a computer, filling a template, and printing it out.

RideBill Pro was built to solve that. A simple, fast Android app that lets operators generate professional GST invoices and maintain trip logs directly from their phone — with no login, no cloud dependency, and no complexity.

Every feature was designed around real workflows: quick trip logging, auto-calculated GST, PDF sharing via WhatsApp, and vehicle-wise history. The result is a tool that works the way transport operators actually work.
