About

Built for the Road

The story behind RideBill Pro — a billing tool designed around how Indian taxi operators actually work.

Why RideBill Pro?

Indian taxi operators — from single cab drivers to multi-vehicle fleet owners — have long relied on paper bill books and manual calculations. When GST compliance became mandatory, the problem got harder: most generic invoice apps don't understand local package rates, hill station surcharges, driver bata, or the difference between personal and corporate trips.

RideBill Pro was built to fix that. Not a generic invoice app with taxi labels slapped on — a billing tool designed from the ground up around the real-world workflow of Indian transport operators.

Privacy First

From day one, the decision was made: no cloud, no login, no account required. All billing data lives on your device only, stored in a local Room database. Your customers' trip details, your company financials, your GSTIN — none of it leaves your phone.

This isn't just a privacy feature. It means the app works in remote hills, on highways with no signal, and in areas with unreliable internet — because it doesn't need any.
