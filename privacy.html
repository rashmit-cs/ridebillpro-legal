Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

RideBill Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information.

Information Storage

RideBill Pro stores bills, trip logs, vehicle information, operator details, and settings locally on your device.

Data Collection

RideBill Pro does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Billing & Subscriptions

If premium plans are purchased, payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing.

Exports

Users may export invoices, PDF files, and Excel reports. Users are responsible for any files shared outside the application.

Data Deletion

The Delete All Data feature permanently removes locally stored application data.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect local data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.
