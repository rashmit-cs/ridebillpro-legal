Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 · Effective for RideBill Pro v1.0.0 and above

Summary in plain language: RideBill Pro stores all your data locally on your device using Room Database. We do not collect any personal information, do not require login, do not track your location, and do not share or sell any data. You have full control over your data at all times.

1. Who We Are

RideBill Pro ("the App") is an Android application developed for transport operators, taxi owners, cab owners, fleet managers, bus operators, and travel agencies. The App is published on the Google Play Store under the package name com.ridebillpro.app.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@ridebillpro.app

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

RideBill Pro is designed as a fully offline, privacy-first application. We explicitly do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • GPS location or device location data
  • Contacts from your phone
  • SMS or call log data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID)
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics
  • Payment card information
  • Photos or media from your gallery (unless you explicitly choose a logo image)

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you enter in the App is stored exclusively on your device using Android Room Database (SQLite). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export and share it yourself.

The following information is stored locally:

  • Company Profile: Company name, owner name, GSTIN, phone number, email, city, state, address, UPI ID, bank details, logo image path
  • Bills: Invoice data including passenger details, trip details, fare breakdown, GST amounts, payment mode, bill number
  • Trip Logs: Date, passenger name, pickup/drop points, vehicle number, amount, time
  • Vehicle Records: Vehicle name, registration number, type, fuel type
  • Subscription Status: Your current plan (Free or Pro), expiry date, and bill count for the current month — stored locally to enforce plan limits

4. Permissions We Request

The App requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • INTERNET — Required for Google Play In-App Billing (subscription purchase verification) and in-app update checks. No personal data is transmitted.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android ≤ 9 only) — Required to save exported PDF files to your device storage.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android ≤ 12 only) — Required to read an image file if you choose to set a company logo.
  • VIBRATE — Used for haptic feedback on button interactions. No data involved.

The App does not request permissions for: Camera, Microphone, Contacts, Location, Phone, SMS, Bluetooth, or Biometrics.

5. Google Play Billing

The Pro subscription is processed through Google Play Billing. When you make a purchase:

  • The payment transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive, store, or process your payment card information.
  • Google Play returns a purchase token, which is stored locally on your device to verify your subscription status.
  • Google's own Privacy Policy applies to the billing transaction. See policies.google.com/privacy.

6. PDF and File Export

When you export a bill or trip log as a PDF, the file is generated entirely on your device using the iText library. The file is saved to your device storage and can be shared via any app you choose (WhatsApp, Email, etc.). We have no access to exported files or where you share them.

7. Internet Usage

The App uses an internet connection only for:

  • Google Play In-App Billing — to process subscription purchases
  • Google Play In-App Update — to notify you of available app updates
  • Google Play In-App Review — to display the app rating prompt

None of your billing data, trip logs, or personal information is transmitted over the internet.

8. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. Within the App, under Settings → Delete All Data, you can permanently erase all bills, trip logs, vehicle records, and company profile data from your device in a single action. This action is irreversible.

Uninstalling the App from your device will also remove all locally stored data.

9. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer any user data to third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to it in the first place.

10. Children's Privacy

RideBill Pro is intended for use by adults operating transport businesses. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.

11. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device protected with a PIN, pattern, or biometric lock. The App's database is stored in the app's private storage directory, which is not accessible to other apps without root access.

12. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and publish an updated version on the Play Store listing. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how RideBill Pro handles data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@ridebillpro.app
  • Website: https://rashmit-cs.github.io/ridebillpro-legal/
Google Play Data Safety Declaration: For the Play Store Data Safety section, declare: No data collected from users. Data is not shared with third parties. Data is not sold. Users can request deletion via Settings → Delete All Data within the app.
