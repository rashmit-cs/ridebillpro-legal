Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 · RideBill Pro (com.ridebillpro.app)

Introduction

RideBill Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how RideBill Pro handles information when you use our Android application.

RideBill Pro is designed as a fully offline, privacy-first application. No personal data is transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

Information Stored Locally

RideBill Pro stores the following information locally on your device only, using Android Room Database:

  • Company Name
  • Owner Name
  • GSTIN (GST Identification Number)
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • City, State, and Address
  • Bill and Invoice Records
  • Trip Logs
  • Vehicle Records
  • Subscription Status

All of this data remains on your device at all times. It is never uploaded, synced, or shared with anyone.

Data We Do NOT Collect

RideBill Pro does not collect, access, or transmit:

  • GPS Location or device location
  • Contacts or phone book data
  • SMS or call logs
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Advertising ID)
  • Browsing history or app usage data
  • Any biometric data

Third-Party Services

RideBill Pro does not integrate analytics platforms (e.g. Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics) or advertising SDKs.

Google Play Billing: If you purchase a Pro subscription, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details. Google's privacy policy applies to that transaction: policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Sharing

RideBill Pro does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties under any circumstances.

File Exports

RideBill Pro allows you to export invoices as PDF files and trip logs as Excel/CSV files. These files are saved to your device storage. When you share them (e.g. via WhatsApp), you are responsible for where they are sent. The app has no control over data you voluntarily share externally.

Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all locally stored data at any time using the Delete All Data option in Settings. This action is irreversible and removes all bills, trip logs, vehicle records, and profile information from your device.

Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

RideBill Pro is intended for adult transport professionals and business operators. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own security settings (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure. We do not have access to your data and cannot recover it if lost.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
