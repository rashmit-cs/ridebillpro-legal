FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a GST invoice?
Go to New Bill, select Corporate as the customer type, enter the GSTIN if required, and enable the GST toggle. The app will automatically calculate 5% GST on the base fare and show the full tax breakup in the invoice.
Can I use RideBill Pro without the internet?
Yes — RideBill Pro is fully offline. All data is stored locally on your device using Android Room Database. No internet connection is required for any feature including billing, exports, and analytics.
Do I need to create an account or log in?
No. RideBill Pro does not require a login, account creation, or email registration. Just install and start billing immediately.
How do I export my bills to Excel?
Go to Trip Log → tap the Export button. This exports all trip records as a CSV file compatible with Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, and similar tools. Pro plan required for export.
How do I share a PDF invoice?
Open any bill → tap Share or Print. The PDF will be generated instantly and you can share it via WhatsApp, email, or any other app. Pro plan required for PDF export.
How do I add a new vehicle?
Go to Fleet → Add Vehicle. Enter the vehicle number, type, and model. You can then select it when creating new bills and view vehicle-wise trip history.
How do I upgrade to Pro?
Go to Settings → Subscription → Upgrade to Pro. You'll be directed to Google Play Billing where you can subscribe for ₹99/month. The upgrade takes effect immediately.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
Cancel anytime via Google Play Store → tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → RideBill Pro → Cancel. You retain Pro access until the end of the billing period.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
All locally stored data (bills, trip logs, vehicle records, profile) is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. We recommend exporting your data as CSV or PDF before uninstalling.
How do I set up my company profile?
Go to Settings → Company Profile → tap Edit. Fill in your company name, owner name, GSTIN, address, and contact details. This information will auto-fill on every invoice you generate.
The app is crashing. What should I do?
Try force-stopping the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, try clearing the app cache (Settings → Apps → RideBill Pro → Clear Cache). If still crashing, email us at tech.support.dev@gmail.com with your Android version and device model.
How do I delete all my data?
Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → Delete All Data. This permanently and irreversibly deletes all bills, trip logs, vehicle records, and profile settings from your device. This cannot be undone.
