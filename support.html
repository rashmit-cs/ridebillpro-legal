FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does RideBill Pro require an internet connection? ▼
No. RideBill Pro works completely offline. All your data is stored on your device. The only time the app uses the internet is when you purchase or verify a Pro subscription through Google Play Billing, or when the app checks for available updates.
Do I need to create an account or login? ▼
No account or login is required. Just install the app, set up your company profile once, and you're ready to start generating bills immediately.
Where is my data stored? Is it safe? ▼
All your data — bills, trip logs, vehicle records, and company profile — is stored locally on your device using Room Database (SQLite). It is stored in the app's private storage, which is not accessible to other apps. Your data never leaves your device unless you export and share it yourself.
How do I export a bill as PDF? ▼
Open any bill from the Bills list, tap the Preview button, then tap the Export/Share icon. The PDF is generated on your device and you can save it or share it via WhatsApp, Email, or any other app. PDF export requires the Pro plan.
What is the Free plan limit? ▼
The Free plan allows up to 10 bill generations per calendar month and management of 1 vehicle. The bill count resets at the start of each new month. Trip logs and analytics remain accessible.
How do I upgrade to Pro? ▼
Go to the Plans tab inside the app and tap the Upgrade button. This will open Google Play Billing where you can complete the subscription purchase securely using your Google Play account.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription? ▼
Go to Google Play Store → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → find RideBill Pro and tap Cancel. You retain Pro access until the end of the current billing period.
I lost my phone / changed phone. Can I recover my data? ▼
Since data is stored locally on your device, it is not recoverable from a different device without a manual backup. If you use Google's automatic device backup feature (enabled in Android settings), your app data may be included in that backup depending on your Android version and backup settings. We recommend regularly exporting your bills as PDFs to ensure you have copies.
How do I delete all my data? ▼
Go to Settings (gear icon) → Delete All Data → confirm. This permanently deletes all bills, trip logs, vehicle records, and company profile from your device. This action cannot be undone.
Is RideBill Pro GST compliant? ▼
The app generates invoices with CGST, SGST, and IGST fields based on the values you enter. You are responsible for entering the correct tax rates and ensuring compliance with applicable GST regulations. We recommend consulting a tax professional for specific compliance questions.
The app is not installing or crashing. What should I do? ▼
The app requires Android 7.0 (API 24) or higher. If you're experiencing issues, try: (1) Ensure your Android version is 7.0 or above, (2) Clear the app cache from Android Settings → Apps → RideBill Pro → Clear Cache, (3) Uninstall and reinstall the app, (4) Email us at support@ridebillpro.app with your device model and Android version.
