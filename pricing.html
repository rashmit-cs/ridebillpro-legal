FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to create an account?
No. RideBill Pro requires no login or account creation. Just install and start billing.
Is my data stored on the cloud?
No. All data is stored locally on your device using a Room database. Nothing is sent to any server.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Cancel anytime from Google Play → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → RideBill Pro → Cancel.
What happens to my data if I uninstall?
All locally stored data is deleted. Export your CSV/Excel before uninstalling if you need a backup.
Is GST billing mandatory?
No. GST is optional. For personal trips, generate clean invoices without any GST applied.
Can I use this app offline?
Yes — fully. No internet is ever required. Works in remote hills, highways, anywhere.
