2. Description of the App

RideBill Pro is a transport billing and invoice management application for Android. It allows users to:

  • Generate GST-compliant transport invoices
  • Maintain trip logs and fleet vehicle records
  • Export bills and logs as PDF files
  • View analytics and revenue summaries

All data is stored locally on the user's device. The App does not require user registration or account creation.
